A Little Bit About Me!

For more than 5 years now, design has been the central
piece of my world. On this fact and mind-blowing journey, I
have moved over the years from being a visual designer to
a full-time UI/UX thinker and designer.

At the moment, the journey has brought me to US mobile
where I am full-time senior Product Designer. In this
position, as with freelance, I am working remotely and I
have been for approximately two years.

With the constant goal of pushing design boundaries and
maintaining a high standard for usability & user experience,
I have consistently worked with various startups and
clients from all around the world and helped them create
amazing and engaging digital products.
